I. Organize by Job Openings

Enter the title of your open position, your subsequent applying candidates will be added to the position.

II. Continually include Candidates

Add candidates that have applied to the position by uploading their resumes in PDF format. Each resume will be stored and processed for required information.

III. Find your ideal Candidate

Identify the candidates that you need by using keywords, you can also filter by their level of education, and look through any of your job openings. Download the resume of any candidate or remove them from your collection.
